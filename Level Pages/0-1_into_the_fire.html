0-1: INTO THE FIRE


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

In a small chamber above the entrance of the first hallway. Accessed by breaking the glass pane above the exit door of the Piercer Room, or the vent in the hallway.

SECRET #2 - SOUL ORB

In the third glass room, a series of pipes above the entrance can be walked on to access it.

SECRET #3 - SOUL ORB

In the curved path after leaving the room containing secret #2, accessed by wall jumping to a hidden opening above the door to the next room.

SECRET #4 - SOUL ORB

In the pipe room, visible from the upper, glowing red corner. Accessed by performing a series of wall jumps.

SECRET #5 - SOUL ORB

In the room just before encountering the Malicious Face. By standing on the middle platform and then facing the door to the pipe room, a grate can be seen just below it. Shooting the grate will destroy it and allow access to the final Orb. Taking the soul orb will spawn 12 Filth behind it.

CHALLENGE

In the second or third of the glass rooms, stay midair above a pane without killing any Filth, and proceed to destroy it once enough have gathered on top.
