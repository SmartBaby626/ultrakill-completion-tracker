0-4: A ONE-MACHINE ARMY


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

In the Statue Arena, a small alcove hides a soul orb near the ceiling above the left platform.

SECRET #2 - BLOOD ORB

Defeating the Malicious Faces in the secret encounter (see below) will allow V1 to obtain the Blood Orb.

SECRET #3 - SOUL ORB

In the Fan Arena, a small platform around the fan pit is hidden beneath the glass floor; a small alcove containing a Soul Orb is hidden on the side closest to the arena's entrance.


SECRET ENCOUNTER

In the Spinning Hallway, a door can be accessed through the window to the right, leading to a small room with a Blood Orb on the other side; approaching it will cause the door to close, and two Malicious Faces will spawn on either side of the door.


CHALLENGE

After clearing out all of the enemies in the mission, return to the beginning and align V1 as straight as possible before sliding; if the Malicious Face's corpse from the Statue Arena is in the way, V1's Ground Slam can destroy them.

MAKE SURE that none of the glass in the center of the Fan Arena is destroyed. You may avoid breaking the glass by using a non-destructive (a weapon that may not break fragile things like glass) weapon and/or luring enemies to the doorway and using the Malicious Railcannon on the crowd. However, use it with caution, as if the explosion is too close to the glass, it may break some of the glass. If you mess up, just pause and press Checkpoint. It will bring you back to before the Fan Arena.
