0-5: CERBERUS


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------


CHALLENGE

The Cerberi can be hit by their explosive orbs; get them to near-death, then evade them until they end up destroying themselves. In other words, just get them down to really low health, then let them kill themselves. Fight the bosses normally at first, making an effort to keep them at roughly the same health. Once both of them are at low health, focus on dodging around them and baiting out their ball-throwing attack. When they start the wind up for that attack, get in close range and dash out at the last second to make them damage themselves. Repeat until both Cerberi have fallen.
