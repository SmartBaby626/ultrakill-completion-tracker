1-1: HEART OF THE SUNRISE


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

In the room with the Red Pedestal, walk up the collapsed column through a discolored panel.

SECRET #2 - SOUL ORB

Through the door to the left of the Red Pedestal, at the top of the tall stone structure.

SECRET #3 - BLOOD ORB

Under the waterfall; approaching it will summon 3 Drones.

SECRET #4 - SOUL ORB

In the Nailgun hallway, behind the darkened stained glass window.

SECRET #5 - SOUL ORB

In a secret cavity in the large hall before the last arena, found by climbing up the wall to the immediate right of the blue door.

CHALLENGE (SECRET LEVEL)

This level's challenge (Complete the level in under 10 seconds) can be completed easily by using the Marksman Revolver to toss a coin into the fountain at the beginning of the level, which will reveal the secret exit that leads to 1-S: THE WITLESS.

SLAB #1

In the room that has the level exit, the roof has 2 glass panes that can be broken with the Core Eject Shotgun grenade or any other weapon that has enough firepower. By performing a slam bounce you can go to the roof of the room and find the first Slab needed for unlocking the Alternate Revolver.
