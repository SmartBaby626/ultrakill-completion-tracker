1-2: THE BURNING WORLD


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

In a secret cavity in the ceiling of the upper floor Arena.

SECRET #2 - SOUL ORB

In the Blue Skull room on the upper floor, a passage under the stairs leads to a set of electric coils.

SECRET #3 - SOUL ORB

In the Red Skull room, through a hole in the right wall above the stone structure.

SECRET #4 - BLOOD ORB

In a crawlspace beneath the lower floor; accessible through a hole next to the stairs. Approaching the Orb will cause the Cerberus above to awaken.

SECRET #5 - SOUL ORB

In the lava room on the lower floor, requiring only simple air control.

CHALLENGE

Requires the Electric Railcannon; taking the path to the second Soul Orb, firing towards the coils will activate them, opening the trapdoor and leading directly into the lower floor, skipping both Skulls.

SLAB #2

In the lava room on the lower floor, look to the left of the staircase. Destroy the wall that's cracked with a Core Eject Shotgun grenade and enter through. At the other end of this hallway thing, you might catch a glimpse of a crack in the wall. Break the crack and enter the dungeon, which is a reference to the YouTuber Civvie11. "Fight" Cancerous Rodent and then go back out of the area. When you go back, you'll encounter Very Cancerous Rodent, who can kill you. In the hallway, the slab room will unlock, go into it and you have slab 2.
