1-3: HALLS OF SACRED REMAINS


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

In the first room, break the glass and climb out of the room to find a Soul Orb.

SECRET #2 - BLOOD ORB

The chute leading down to the Blue Skull room holds a Blood Orb.

SECRET #3 - SOUL ORB

In the Lava Stairs room, a secret cavity beneath the lowest stairs hides a Soul Orb.

SECRET #4 - SOUL ORB

Next to the Blue Skull, sliding into where the waterfall emerges from the wall lets V1 slip into a secret room containing a Soul Orb.

SECRET #5 - SOUL ORB

In the Courtyard Arena, sliding up a slope next to the exit door will let V1 slip into a crack in the wall, leading to a Soul Orb.

CHALLENGE (SECRET ENCOUNTER)

After returning to the start area and placing both skulls, a secret glass pane door opens at the back, leading you to a secret boss fight against Swordsmachine AGONY and Swordsmachine TUNDRA.

SLAB #3

After beating the Secret Encounter, a door opens in the back of the arena, revealing Slab III.
