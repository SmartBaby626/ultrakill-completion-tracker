2-1: BRIDGEBURNER


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

After going into the second water room, turn to face the ramp and break the cracked wall to the left; this will lead out of the level, with a Soul Orb on a suspended platform.

SECRET #2 - SOUL ORB

A Soul Orb can be found on a Bounce Pad just beneath the first platform on the Bridge.

SECRET #3 - BLOOD ORB

A Blood Orb can be seen in the distance on the Bridge; get to the first platform by dashing onto a jump platform beneath the second arena, then use the Pump Charge Shotgun's overpump to reach the last platform. Alternatively, you can use an Ultradive to skip this and get there without self-damage. Additionally, Rocket Surfing over to the platform with the use of the Freezeframe Rocket Launcher works, though depending on the angle where you shot the rocket, you may also need to dash as to not make V1 fall to its doom.

SECRET #4 - SOUL ORB

A Soul Orb can be found at the end of the Bridge; after hitting the checkpoint, turn around and slide down the side of the previous platform, then dash onto the lower platform.

SECRET #5 - SOUL ORB

Before dropping down to the bridge leading to the exit door, jump to the platform to the left of Building B's exit door; around the side of the building is the last Soul Orb.

CHALLENGE

In the room with the first door, look behind the staircase. You'll see a crack in the wall. Destroy it with the Knuckleblaster, Core Eject, or Alternate Revolver. Go through the hole and jump onto the roof. Get an overpump ready, and then wall jump around the tower and onto the bridge, use the explosion to propel yourself if you need to. Continue through the level as normal until you get to the place before the second tower. Get an overpump ready and jump around the tower and use the explosion to get onto one of the platforms, and voila, you completed the challenge. Alternatively, wait until the Freezeframe Rocket Launcher is obtained and use chain surfing to bypass the doors.
