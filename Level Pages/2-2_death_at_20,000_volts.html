2-2: DEATH AT 20,000 VOLTS


SECRETS GUIDE

---------------------------------

SECRET #1 - SOUL ORB

In the first block, the first Soul Orb peeks out of a weak window above the Cerberus. It leads to a room with a praying skeleton.

SECRET #2 - SOUL ORB

In the Electric Hallway, jump over to the platform where the three Soldiers spawn; going to the left a Soul Orb can be found at the bottom of a short drop.

SECRET #3 - BLOOD ORB

A Blood Orb can be found in the same section by jumping on top of the hallway right before the entrance of the Railcannon room.

SECRET #4 - BLOOD ORB

>

In the pool of water behind the row of Husks in the second plaza, V1 can slide into a small crack to enter a small secret waterway, where a second Blood Orb can be found.

SECRET #5 - SOUL ORB

In the building that contains the book, a hole is blasted in the wall on the second floor. The cracked wall is visible through this hole, and can be broken to reveal a creepy altar as well as the Soul Orb.

CHALLENGE

Just learn the Movement Guide and memorize the electrical box locations. The shotgun can be used for boxes at medium range because one of the pellets is bound to hit.

The box inside the house in the First Block can be destroyed by shooting the house from outside with the Malicious Railcannon (or any explosion, really). It's most consistent to target the wall of the house next to the Cerberus, though it may be faster to fire an explosion at the window instead.

Beating the level in under 60 seconds may be hard starting out, but if you just keep replaying the level and memorizing everything you're good to go. If you're not a fan of memorizing, then just try to beat it as fast as possible. You can also skip some enemy areas such as the Generator Staircase so that you can go through the level faster.

Try to remember the location of all the electrical boxes. When you have figured that out, rush the level, ignoring all enemies (except for an occasional refuel, if needed). Using the overload blast of the Pump Charge Shotgun or Rocket Riding can be especially useful in the Generator Staircase, but keep in mind that the shotgun's blast does 50 damage to you.
