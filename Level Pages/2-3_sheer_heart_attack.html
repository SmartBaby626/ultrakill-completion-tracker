SECRET MISSION



The Secret Level Exit is behind the waterfall in the Waterfall Arena, but the water pressure is too powerful for V1 to slip through.

There are three electric boxes similar to those in [ 2-2: DEATH AT 20,000 VOLTS ] hidden throughout the level. When destroyed, they will drain all water and disable all deadly equipment in the level. This allows V1 to pass through the waterfall and reach the Secret Level Exit to [ 2-S: ALL-IMPERFECT LOVE SONG ].

The locations of the breakers are listed below.

(Electric Box 1) - Under the bridge in the Waterfall Arena.
(Electric Box 2) - In a hidden area near the ceiling of the Grinder Arena.
(Electric Box 3) - Behind the stairs leading to the Red Skull pedestal.

CHALLENGE

As both Level Exits are blocked by waterfalls, it is impossible to complete this challenge without draining the water; careful platforming allows V1 to destroy each of the breakers without touching the water. The underwater route to the Grinder Arena can be skipped by blowing up a cracked wall in the Lobby, leading directly to the Blue Skull pedestal.
